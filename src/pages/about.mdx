<h1 class="text-3xl md:text-5xl font-serif text-zinc-900 dark:text-white leading-tight mb-8">
Hi, I'm Ruben. I build <em class="text-emerald-600 dark:text-[#4ade80] not-italic">CAD software and geometric algorithms</em>, and write about the craft.
</h1>

I'm a software engineer at **Bricsys** in Ghent, Belgium, where I work on the geometric modeling kernel of BricsCAD. My day-to-day is C++ — computational geometry, Boolean operations, surface intersections, and the kind of algorithmic problems that keep you up at night (in a good way).

Before CAD, I built cutting and optimization algorithms for industrial food processing at MARELEC, and before that I did high-voltage research at ABB in Switzerland — testing circuit breakers, building signal processing pipelines, and learning that hardware has a way of humbling you.

I studied electromechanical engineering at UGent with an Erasmus year in Grenoble, and I'm currently doing a postgraduate in Applied AI at Howest.
